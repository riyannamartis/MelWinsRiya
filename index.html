Our Story

We’re so thankful that we found each other, and are thrilled that you will be joining us to celebrate our love and future together.

  • In A Relationship

    December 21, 2020

    Even though we started as “just friends” it soon became clear that we were meant to be so much more than that. Our first, undeniable spark turned into a unique and wonderful bond that only soul mates can have.
    - Riyanna

  • First Date

    August 1, 2021

    After almost 7 months of long-distance relationship, we finally met in Canada. After my hotel quarantine period, Melwyn came to visit me for the first time. I was looking out the hotel window & searching eagerly for his car. ... When he reached, I ran down the stairs with butterflies in my stomach. I approached towards him as he got out of his car with a bouquet of flowers in his hand. He had the sweetest, most genuine smile on his face & I still have an exact memory of it to this day. Melwyn took me out for lunch and then we spent the rest of the day at a park together, chatting and getting to know each other even better. We had pretty much everything in common. He checked everything on my compatibility list, not to mention he was incredibly funny. I had honestly never been that smitten with anyone in my life. Tall, tan skin, dark hair, gorgeous brown eyes. Check, check, check & check! Besides having everything in common & having a ridiculously beautiful smile, he was most importantly, kind. I believe you can tell when someone is genuinely nice, kind & respectful within minutes. Melwyn was a gentleman and on top of all that, he had a great sense of humor. ​
    - Riyanna

  • The Proposal

    February 12, 2022

    I wanted to propose to Riyanna in a special and memorable way and so I decided to ask for her hand in marriage on her birthday in the picturesque surrounding of Blue Mountains. I booked a skiing trip for us and planned to pop the question ... when the moment was right. We ended up completing our first skiing session and then as we were walking the village I found just the perfect spot – an ice sculpture that read "L❤️VE". It was freezing cold and it wasn’t helping my nerves but I went down on my knee, pulled out the ring and asked the love of my life, “Will you marry me?” and she said YES with tears in her eyes.
    - Melwyn

  • The Civil Marriage

    April 11, 2022

    Once Riyanna became my fiancé I could not wait to start our journey together. January 2023 was too far, and we decided why wait, when we can be united as husband and wife, right here in our new home - Canada. ... I applied for our marriage licence and got a date in April. Witnessed by our friends, we had the loveliest civil marriage ceremony at the City Hall in London, Ontario. My bride looked so pretty in her white dress that I couldn’t take my eyes of her. I had never seen her so happy and I think her joy was transcended only by mine and from that day onwards I would call her ‘My Better Half’.
    - Melwyn
